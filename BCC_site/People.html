People

Among us you shall find physiotherapists, speech therapists, language therapists, doctors and volounteers. We work with children who have moderate to severe learning and physical disabilities and sensory impairments – typically autism, cerebral palsy and muscular dystrophy. We also encounter rare syndromes and conditions. Referral into the Children with Disabilities Social Work Team comes via the Safeguarding Referral Team (SRT) and provides a Children and Families Assessment which may lead to the provision of an allocated Social Worker or Social Work Assistant who will draw up a Child In Need Plan to offer Focused and/or Enhanced Social Care Services. These services are coordinated in order to support Stoke on Trent's vision for children and young people as set out in the Children & Young People's Plan is for them
